03 Research
Problem &
Solutions
Problem

Most fitness apps overwhelm beginners with unfiltered content. There is no way to say "I am at home today" and get a workout that actually fits. Progress tracking is vague. Challenges feel arbitrary.

Solution

Libo structures all 502 exercises by environment and body focus. 30-day challenges scale your reps daily so the app always knows today's target. The Alt Scanner gives 3 equipment-free alternatives to any exercise, instantly.
