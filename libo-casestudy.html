What is
Libo?

Libo is an all-in-one fitness training club designed for real life. Whether you're at home, in the gym, or on the go — Libo delivers expert-built workouts, personalised training plans, and smart tools to keep you moving every single day.

Built for the 18–35 market across Europe with a focus on Germany, Libo combines the best of premium training apps with zero barriers to entry — free to start, no equipment required.
